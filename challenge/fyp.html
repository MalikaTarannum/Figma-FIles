Admissions Open for Fall Semester 2023

All the applicants whose names appear on the first merit lists are requested to observe the following guidelines to secure admission:

  • Download and print the Admission Fee Challan from the admission portal (118.107.134.251/admissions).
  • Deposit the fee at designated banks mentioned in the Fee Challan
  • Candidates can deposit fees in any branch of JS Bank and BankIslamic across Pakistan
  • The deadline for fee submission is September 01, 2023
  • Submit department copy of the admission fee slip at the Admission Office UOBS City Campus
  • Regular classes will start from Monday, August 28, 2023
